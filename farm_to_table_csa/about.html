About

Mission + Vision

At Farm to Table CSA in Elberta, Alabama, our mission is to nurture our community through USDA organic farming and the Community Supported Agriculture model. We pledge to promote sustainability by supporting local agriculture and providing the freshest produce directly to our customers.

Company History

Farm to Table CSA is a family-run farm located in Elberta, Alabama. We grow our produce using USDA organic, non-GMO seeds and heirloom plants. We offer seasonal CSA (Community Supported Agriculture) shares during the fall and spring. By joining, members directly support local agriculture, eliminating the middleman.

Values + Sustainability

We prioritize organic growing practices, seasonal harvesting, and community-first partnerships. Our CSA model reduces food miles, supports local families, and keeps farming sustainable long-term.
